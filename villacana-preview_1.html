The Resort

Life at Villacana

Situated on the beachfront between Estepona and San Pedro, Villacana is a whitewashed village built around beautifully kept gardens and four swimming pools — all just metres from the Mediterranean.

The resort is pedestrian-only, making it safe and peaceful for families. A short walk brings you to local restaurants in Cancelada; a short drive to the marina and nightlife of Puerto Banús.

  • Direct beach access
  • 4 swimming pools
  • Pedestrian resort
  • Manicured gardens
  • Near Puerto Banús
  • Year-round sunshine
  • Family-friendly
  • Key-access pools
